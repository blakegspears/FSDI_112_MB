About Me

My name is Blake Spears. I am 30 years old and I live in Elk River, Minnesota. I was born and raised in Louisiana. At the age of 22, I joined the U.S. Navy and served as an Aviation Electrician's Mate at HSC-23 on Naval Air Station North Island, Coronado, California. I met my wife in San Diego, and we have a beautiful 6 Year old daughter. I completed my 2nd enlistment at 6 years and transferred to the reserves to be with my family. After a year and a half, I completed my reserve enlistment and am now a machine operator and CDL driver for a construction company. Some hobbies I have are outdoor activities and DIY projects.
